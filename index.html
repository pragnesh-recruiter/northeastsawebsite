What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive, conflict-free financial advisory across corporate finance, capital markets, and strategic transactions — delivered with full commitment at every step.

01

Corporate Finance

We provide comprehensive corporate finance advisory to businesses at every stage of their growth journey. From structuring and arranging equity and debt capital to advising on mergers, acquisitions, and complex balance sheet transactions, we act as a trusted co-pilot for corporates and SMEs navigating the capital markets. Our services span M&A advisory, equity and debt capital markets, long and short-term debt arrangements, bridge financing, working capital solutions, bond arranging, restructuring advisory, treasury advisory, and balance sheet optimization — delivering end-to-end solutions tailored to each client's unique financial objectives.

Learn More
02

Financial Restructuring Advisory

When businesses face financial stress, complexity, or the need for transformation, NorthEast provides expert restructuring advisory to navigate the path forward. We work with corporates, lenders, and stakeholders to design and implement practical, sustainable restructuring solutions — whether addressing overleveraged balance sheets, NPL challenges, or the need to refinance and recapitalize. Our restructuring expertise spans debt restructuring, liability management, creditor negotiations, balance sheet optimization, and turnaround advisory — always focused on preserving value and restoring financial health for all stakeholders involved.

Learn More
03

Strategic Advisory

Sound financial strategy is the foundation of every successful business decision. Our strategic advisory practice partners with corporates, family businesses, and institutional clients to provide independent, high-quality counsel on the transactions, structures, and opportunities that matter most. Our capabilities include mergers and acquisitions strategy, joint ventures and alliances, rating advisory, private placement structuring, IPO readiness, public-private partnerships, and capital structuring advice — delivering the clarity and conviction clients need to act decisively in an increasingly complex global landscape.

Learn More
04

Investment Advisory

We provide sophisticated investment advisory to a diverse client base — spanning corporates seeking portfolio diversification, private clients building long-term wealth, and ultra-high-net-worth individuals (UHNWIs) requiring bespoke solutions across global markets. Our investment advisory practice identifies and structures access to local and international investment opportunities, emerging and frontier markets, cross-border transactions, and leveraged equity solutions. We do not manage assets — we provide independent, conflict-free advice that connects our clients to the right opportunities, structured optimally for their objectives, risk appetite, and time horizon.

Meet Our Advisor

Nikunj Patel

17+ Years Experience
UAE & Global Markets
USD 1.25B+ Capital Raised
Chartered Accountant & Certified Business Valuer

Nikunj Patel is a corporate finance professional with over 17 years of experience in fundraising, real estate Financial structuring, debt restructuring, and strategic finance. He has successfully led capital-raising transactions exceeding USD 1.25 billion from capital markets, including the largest-ever Green Sukuk issued by a UAE real estate developer — listed on the London Stock Exchange and NASDAQ Dubai — and bilateral banking facilities in excess of USD 2 billion.

Prior to founding NorthEast Strategic Advisors, Nikunj served as Chief Financial Officer of a multi-billion-dollar real estate & construction group, rated by leading international credit rating agencies, where he played a pivotal role in shaping the company's financial strategy, capital structure, and funding initiatives.

He is a qualified Chartered Accountant and Certified Business Valuer from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, and is the Founder & Managing Partner of NorthEast Strategic Advisors — a UAE-based boutique financial advisory firm specialising in capital raising, corporate finance, restructuring, and strategic advisory services.
